Background

Little Botany started as an instagram name, @lilbotany. Fond of hip-hop and rap, Rachel kept coming across musicians with the prefix 'lil'. What first started as a tongue-in-cheek nod, soon developed into a brand. Rachel's portfolio started expanding from botanical illustrations into album artwork for artists like Father, Archibald Slim, and more.
